Fun Fact: I love surfing!


There are many reasons why I love surfing (and not just because I come from one of surfing's most iconic cities).

  1. The Culture
    • Surfers are known for being layed back and welcoming.
    • While a spirit of competitiveness does exist, most surfers are always stoked to help one another out and make new friends.

  2. The Sport
    • One of my favorite aspects about the sport is that it is completly free!(well, after you buy a $250 wetsuit and $500+ surfboard). Say goodbye to expensive annual passes!
    • Not only is it free, but also quite safe. Unlike most other extreme sports that often result in great injury or pain, surfing's biggest wipeouts are often stress-free due to the soft water.

  3. The Experience
    • For me, going for a surf session is an entire experience. Everything from the smell of fresh wax to the warming feeling of the sun on my face makes the experience one of a kind.
    • Surfing serves as an excellent form of excercise as it requires great effort from the shoulers, arms, back, and lungs.
    • Because I grew up surfing, every time I paddle out I am reminded me of my youthful upbringing and can't help but feel of joyful nostalgia.


If you would like to watch a pro surfer in his element, check out this YouTube video of Hawaii Native John Florence. John is known for having one of the best, most fluid styles on the World Tour and has already won the championship twice.
